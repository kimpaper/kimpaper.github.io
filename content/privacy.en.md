This site (`kimpaper.github.io`) respects your privacy. Here is how data and
cookies are handled.

## 1. What we collect

This site has no sign-up and does not directly collect or store personally
identifying information. Anonymous usage statistics and cookies may be
collected through the third-party services below.

## 2. Cookies

Cookies may be used to improve the site and serve ads. You can block or delete
cookies in your browser settings.

## 3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand anonymous visit patterns (pages viewed,
time on site). Opt out: [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## 4. Google AdSense

We serve ads via Google AdSense. Third-party vendors including Google use
cookies to show personalized ads based on prior visits to this and other sites.
Opt out at [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or
[www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

## 5. Third parties

No personal data is sold or shared beyond the services listed above.
